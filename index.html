Welcome to Mr. Mannings Coding Class

I am not here for a paycheck, and you are not here for a grade. We are here to develop the ability to communicate with computers. This is no easy task. You have to learn how to think like a computer. You have a lot of vocabulary and rules to learn. You must become extremely detail orientated. And you must learn to overcome the frustration of failure that is a part of developing any significant program. Becoming a computer programmer is a long journey, but one worth taking. With our abilities we can create games and art, we can solve problems, and we can hack the technological world around us.

- Mr. Manning

Announcements

Dear Students,

I was moving too fast towards a project I had in mind to complete by the end of the year. This was causing a lot of confusion and frustration. And for that I apologize. We are starting again at Javascript Unit 1 with shapes. From now on there are no timeframes. Complete the units at your own pace (doing nothing is not a pace). My lessons will be more in depth, so that you understand the code you are typing. But that means you need to read the guides I make. And don't expect to understand something the first time you try it.
